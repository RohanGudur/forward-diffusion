The Forward Diffusion Process

The forward diffusion process is a technique used in diffusion models where noise is gradually added to an image at each step. This helps the model learn to reverse this process during training to generate new images. Explore the steps below to visualize how noise increases with each step.

Understanding the Process

As you move the slider above, you'll notice the gradual increase in noise, simulating the forward diffusion process. This noise addition helps in training deep learning models to reverse the noise, ultimately generating high-quality images from random noise.

In practice, this process is key for generative models, including diffusion models that aim to create high-quality, realistic images from scratch.
